===== DESCRIPTION =====

Todo Budget is a free Android app that combines four essential productivity tools into one lightweight package:

📋 **Task Manager** — Priorities, subtasks, recurring tasks, swipe gestures, reminders, search
💰 **Budget Tracker** — Income/expense tracking by category, spending limits, debt tracking, charts, home screen widget
⏱ **Pomodoro Timer** — Customizable focus/break intervals, session statistics
📝 **Quick Notes** — Text + voice input

**Why it's different:**
- 100% free — no subscriptions, no premium, no in-app purchases. Ever.
- 9 MB — lighter than a single photo
- Works completely offline
- All data stored locally — nothing leaves your device
- Built with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Material 3

**The problem it solves:**
Most people need 3-4 apps for tasks, budget, and focus timer. Each one costs $3-5/month. Todo Budget replaces all of them — for free.

Available on RuStore and as a direct APK download.
